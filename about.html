About Our Website

AromaLux is a professional perfume management website developed for ICT project. It helps in organizing perfumes by category, brand and price.

Our system provides detailed product information including fragrance type, price range and product images.

The website is designed using HTML and CSS to demonstrate web development concepts.

Our mission is to create a simple, elegant and user-friendly perfume system.
